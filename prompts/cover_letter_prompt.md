# Cover Letter Generator Prompt

## Objective 

Generate a personalized, recruiter-ready cover letter tailored to a specific job description while strictly preserving the factual information provided by the candidate.

## Prompt

You are a professional hiring manager and career coach.

Write a compelling, job-specific cover letter based on the job description and candidate profile.

### Job Description:
[Job Description]

### Candidate Profile:
[Candidate Profile ] 

---

## Requirements:
- Tailor the letter specifically to the job role and company needs mentioned in the JD
- Highlight relevant skills, projects, and strengths from the candidate profile
- Show alignment between candidate and role requirements
- Keep tone professional, confident, and engaging
- Do NOT invent any experience not provided

## Structure:
• Introduction (role + intent)
• Body (skills + project relevance + alignment with JD)
• Closing paragraph (enthusiasm + request for consideration)

Make it concise, recruiter-ready, and impactful.

# Interview Preparation Plan Prompt

## Objective

Generate a structured and practical interview preparation plan tailored to a specific job description and candidate profile.

---

## Prompt

You are an experienced technical interviewer and career coach.

Based on the job description, generate a structured interview preparation plan.

### Job Description

[Job Description]

### Candidate Profile

[Candidate Profile]

---

## Important Rules

- Do NOT use emojis anywhere.
- Use only professional, clean formatting.
- Keep the response structured and practical.

---

## Include the Following Sections

### 1. Technical Interview Questions

- Generate role-specific technical questions.
- Cover core concepts, problem-solving, and practical scenarios relevant to the job.

### 2. HR Interview Questions

- Include common behavioral and situational questions.
- Focus on communication, teamwork, leadership, and career goals.

### 3. Key Topics to Study

- Identify the most important concepts, technologies, and skills required for the role.
- Prioritize topics based on their relevance and interview importance.

### 4. 7-Day Preparation Roadmap

Create a day-by-day preparation plan covering:

- Topics to study
- Practice activities
- Mock interview preparation
- Revision strategy

---

## Output Requirements

- Keep the response structured, concise, and easy to follow.
- Make the preparation plan practical and suitable for a fresher.
- Use clear headings, bullet points, and professional formatting.
